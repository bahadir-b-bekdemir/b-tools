B-Tools Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

B-Tools does not collect, transmit, or sell any user data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

Local Storage

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser:

  • Preferences: Language, theme, and UI settings
  • Tool history: Recent actions (e.g., screenshots, color picks) with page URLs—used only to display history in the popup
  • Sticky notes: Notes you create on web pages
  • Temporary data: Screenshots and images while editing—cleared when you close the editor

Third Parties

B-Tools does not share data with third parties. No analytics, tracking, or advertising is used.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, use the support link on the Chrome Web Store listing.
